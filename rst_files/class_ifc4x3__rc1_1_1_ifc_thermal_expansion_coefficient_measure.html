class Ifc4x3_rc1::IfcThermalExpansionCoefficientMeasure : public IfcUtil::IfcBaseType¶

IfcThermalExpansionCoeffientMeasure is a measure of the thermal expansion coefficient of a material, which expresses its elongation (as a ratio) per temperature difference. It is usually measured in 1/K. A positive elongation per (positive) rise of temperature is expressed by a positive value. Type: REAL
